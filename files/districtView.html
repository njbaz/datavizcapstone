District-Level Analysis

Use this dashboard to explore and analyze district performance trends, learning loss, recovery, and demographic data. The first section provides a visual timeline of scores from 2016 to 2023, showing how performance compares to the national 5th-grade average and highlighting changes over time, including the impact of COVID-19. The second section focuses on learning loss between 2019 and 2022, followed by partial recovery through 2023, offering insights into academic progress. The third section offers a district profile, including racial demographics, minority enrollment levels, district size, and free lunch eligibility. Users can filter by district and subject (Math or Reading Language Arts) to tailor the data to their specific needs.
