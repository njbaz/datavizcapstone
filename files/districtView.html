Tool #3: District Dashboard

This dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of district-level performance, focusing on academic scores, learning loss, and recovery trends. Users can analyze performance data by selecting a specific district and subject, enabling customized views of educational outcomes. Section #1 visualizes scores by year (GYS scores), showing changes relative to national averages and assigning letter grades for clarity. Section #2 highlights learning loss during the pandemic (2019-2022) and subsequent recovery efforts (2022-2023) using bar charts for quick comparisons. In Section #3, users can explore demographic profiles, including race/ethnicity breakdowns, minority enrollment, and economic indicators like free lunch eligibility. A district map provides geographic context, while percentile rankings help benchmark performance against other California districts. This snapshot allows educators, administrators, and policymakers to identify trends, pinpoint gaps, and evaluate recovery efforts to guide data-driven decisions.
